Fajita Chicken Burrito Bowl

Description

This fajita chicken burrito bowl is healthy, flavourful and can be put together quickly. It's perfect to meal prep for lunches through the week.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 2tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2tsp chilli flakes
  • 1tsp ground cumin
  • Large handful of roughly chopped coriander
  • 2 large boneless chicken thighs sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into strips
  • Large handful of lettuce leaves
  • 200g black beans
  • 200g sweetcorn
  • 1 avocado peeled, deseeded and sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large shallow bowl combine lime juice, oil, garlic, cumin, chilli flakes, corainder, salt & pepper. Mix together.
  2. Add the sliced chicken thighs and mix so that the chicken is well-coated. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a large pan on medium-high heat and add the oil. Add the chicken and sear until golden and cooked through. Transfer to a warm plate.
  4. Add the peppers to the pan and cook until soft and slightly charred. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Arrange the other ingredients into bowls along with rice that has been cooked to your taste.
  6. Top with the chicken and peppers and any remaining coriander.
