Chickpea Curry

Description

This rich, creamy and satisfying chickpea curry has amazing flavour and is so quick to make. Perfect for a healthy weeknight meal or to prepare ahead. It's plant-based, gluten and dairy free!

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 240g tinned chickpeas
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 1tsp garam masala
  • 1tsp turmeric
  • 1/2tsp ginger powder or paste
  • 1/2tsp chilli flakes
  • 1/2 medium-sized onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2tbsp olive oil
  • 20g fresh baby spinach, rinsed
  • Handful of fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 2 lemon wedges
  • Sea salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan. When the oil is smoking hot, lower the heat and add the garam masala, turmeric, ginger powder and chilli flakes
  2. After a few seconds, add the chopped onions and minced garlic.
  3. Once the onion and garlic start to brown (after 1 min), add the chickpeas and coconut milk. Add salt to taste.
  4. Stir together and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the spinach and wilt for 2 minutes.
  6. Squeeze over the lemon slices and add the coriander. Give it a final stir.
  7. Serve with basmati rice and naan.
